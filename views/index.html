Through out history, there have been many different mythologies that have arose around the world. Some people who are interested in learning more about these mythologies might have a hard time choosing one to start with. This website showcases a few mythologies by sharing some information about them in the hopes of helping someone chose a mythology to get interested in.


Norse Mythology

Norse mythology is a body of myths belonging to the North Germanic people that stems from the Old Norse religion. It is a mythology that involves 9 different realms that surround the world tree, Yggdrasil. Each realm is populated by a different race: Gods, Jötnar (giants and trolls), dwarves, elves, humans, and monsters. Norse mythology has many different events including the destruction and rebirth of the world, known as Ragnarök.
