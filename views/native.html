Native American Legend

The fact and fiction about Native fiction.

The native people were not "religous" as Western culture describes it, but moreso spiritual. Many tales revolved around common characters such as the Apache's Owl, Woodpecker, and Coyote.

Apache Characters

  • Owl, a provider and wise mentor.

  • Woodpecker, a humble and grateful worker.

  • Coyote, a deceiving and quick trickster.

  • Mole, a wise and friendly trickster.
